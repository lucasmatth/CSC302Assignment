The First Data

This data represents the percentage of videos that trends in how many amount of days.

Note that our database consists of only trending videos. Thus, the bar of 0 indicates that the video trends on the same day that it's released.

The x-axis is the number of days between published time and trending time.

The y-axis is the percentage of videos that trends.

The reason why we don't use number of videos instead is because we couldn't scale the y-axis down efficiently
